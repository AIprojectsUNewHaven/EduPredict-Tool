    How EduPredict Pro works

    Three steps from inputs to a defensible GO / NO-GO recommendation. Every number is traceable to a federal data source.

    01

    Set your scenario

    Choose program type, student origin, launch term, scenario (conservative / baseline / optimistic), and target state. 162 validated combinations.

    02

    Models run in parallel

    Enrollment forecaster loads IPEDS 2014–2024 baselines. ROI calculator pulls real BLS wages. Job market analyzer overlays Anthropic AI exposure data.

    03

    Download your report

    Export a PDF with enrollment projections, confidence intervals, ROI breakdown, labor market signals, and a clear STRONG GO / GO / CONDITIONAL / RECONSIDER verdict.

    About

    EduPredict Pro helps College Deans make data-driven decisions about launching AI degree programs.


    Features:

    • 3-year enrollment forecasting
    • AI labor market analysis
    • ROI calculations with risk flags
    • Honest GO/NO-GO recommendations

    Data: BLS 2023, Anthropic Economic Index 2026, IPEDS 2023-2024

    How to read this
    • Step 1: Set program, term, and state, then click Generate forecast.
    • Step 2: Check the top KPIs for size (cohort, total) and viability (confidence, ROI).
    • Step 3: Read the recommendation card first, then review risks and opportunities.
    • Step 4: Use scenario/state charts to stress-test before final decisions.

    Metric glossary
    Cohort: Estimated students in your first intake term.
    Total: Sum of the next 3 yearly intakes.
    Confidence: Model reliability signal. Higher is better.
    ROI ratio: Revenue divided by costs. Above 1.0 means positive return.
    Payback: Estimated time to recover launch costs.
    Break-even: Student count required to avoid loss.
